About Telugu Treats – Where Heritage Meets Indulgence



At Telugu Treats, we don’t just bake cookies—we craft experiences infused with tradition, passion, and authenticity. Rooted in the rich culinary heritage of Andhra Pradesh, every cookie is a celebration of time-honored recipes, perfected across generations.

Our journey began with a simple mission: to bring the flavors of our childhood to life, one cookie at a time. Each bite transports you to a world where the aroma of freshly baked goods fills the air, and the warmth of home is felt in every crumb.

We take pride in using only the finest, all-natural ingredients—premium ghee, jaggery, nuts, and aromatic spices—ensuring that our cookies are not just delicious but also a wholesome treat. Handcrafted with love and care, each batch is a testament to our commitment to quality and authenticity.

Telugu Treats is more than just a brand; it’s a community. We believe in supporting local farmers, businesses, and artisans, ensuring that our cookies not only taste good but also do good. By choosing Telugu Treats, you’re not just indulging in a delightful treat; you’re becoming part of a movement that cherishes and preserves our rich cultural heritage.

Whether you’re savoring a quiet moment with a cup of chai or elevating a grand celebration, Telugu Treats transforms everyday indulgence into a cherished ritual. With each bite, you’re not just tasting a cookie—you’re embracing an heirloom of flavors, a legacy of craftsmanship, and the heartfelt warmth of home.

"Because at Telugu Treats, tradition is not just remembered—it is relished."
