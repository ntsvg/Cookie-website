1.Ragi-biscuits Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup ragi flour
  • 1/2 cup whole wheat flour
  • 1/4 cup jaggery powder
  • 1/2 cup butter or ghee
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • Water as needed
  • Chopped nuts (optional)

Instructions

Follow these steps to make delicious Ragi biscuits:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine ragi flour, whole wheat flour, jaggery powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add butter or ghee and mix until crumbly.
  4. Add water gradually to form a soft dough.
  5. Shape the dough into small biscuits and place them on a baking tray lined with parchment paper.
  6. Sprinkle chopped nuts on top if desired.
  7. Bake for 15-20 minutes or until golden brown.
  8. Let them cool before serving.

Enjoy your healthy and delicious Ragi biscuits!
